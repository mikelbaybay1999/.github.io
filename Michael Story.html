WHO AM I?

I am Michael Glenn Baybay, A 17 years old gal with a very unique way of representing fashion. Small bags are my addiction. While other girls carry big totes and handbags so they can bring everything with them, I prefer compact ones— a bag that won’t hurt my shoulders even if I’m carrying it the whole day. The challenge with that is, I need all my things to be tiny too so I can bring all my necessities and still be comfortable the whole day. Coccinelle is my go-to bag when I’m traveling because of its shape and style. Don’t be deceived with its size— it might look small (just the way I like it), but I was able to bring everything I needed: wallet, portable charger, hand lotion, lipstick, balm, phone, pocket wifi and a lot more! Its suede texture and red shade instantly gives all of my looks an instant pop, especially when I’m wearing black. Comfort and coolness in one bag.

It’s always hard to pack when I’m traveling because I don’t like bringing tons of clothes with me that I know I won’t be able to use. With that, I always make it a point that I pack clothes that have essential colors, simple and easier to mix and match with printed pieces. Don’t get me wrong— I’m obsessed with prints, but bringing comfortable and basic pieces are must-brings when you’re out of the country.
